Kingmaker cantons: who really decides a Swiss federal vote?

A Swiss vote can hinge on one canton — a big one through sheer population, or a small one through the cantonal majority (Ständemehr) that double-majority votes also require. Click a canton to cross it out and watch the national result recompute. Find the canton that flips the vote.
